Road Aware - Beta

Help

Clicking on the "Map" button will take you to a Google Maps page where you can view and log issues. To log an issue simply click on the part of the map the issue and and click on "Log new road issue". "Table Report" button will give a list of all reported road issues.

About and Contact

This reporting system is designed to report issues that cyclists come across while riding. If you find an issue with this page, or would like improvements added please contact myself (Michael Wheeler) at roadaware@mwheeler.org
